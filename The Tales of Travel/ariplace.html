Tourist Places in Ari Atoll

Things To Do

Maamigili Beyru

About

This site is renowned for frequent whale shark sightings, offering divers the chance to swim alongside the world's largest fish. The reef is also home to diverse marine life, including lionfish, moray eels, and various tropical fish species.

Broken Rock

About

Broken Rock is a renowned dive site located in the Dhigurah Channel of South Ari Atoll, Maldives. This unique underwater formation features a canyon that splits the reef into two parts, creating a swim-through experience for divers. The site ranges in depth from 12 to 30 meters and is characterized by strong currents, making it suitable for experienced divers.

Kudarah Thila

About

This circular pinnacle is celebrated for its vibrant marine life, including schools of jacks, snappers, and the occasional reef shark. The site is also known for its dramatic underwater landscapes, making it a favorite among photographers.

Mushimasmigili

About

A renowned site for encountering grey reef sharks, Mushimasmigili also offers sightings of eagle rays and large schools of fish. The site features a rocky reef with vibrant coral growth, providing a dynamic diving experience.

Five Rocks

About

Comprising five prominent pinnacles, this dive site offers encounters with barracudas, eagle rays, and various reef fish. The site is characterized by its strong currents and dramatic underwater topography, providing an exhilarating dive experience.
