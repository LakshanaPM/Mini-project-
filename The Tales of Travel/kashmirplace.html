Tourist Places in Kashmir

Things To Do

Dal Lake, Srinagar

About

Dal Lake is a serene freshwater lake in Srinagar, often referred to as the "Jewel in the Crown of Kashmir." Visitors can enjoy shikara rides, stay on traditional houseboats, and explore floating gardens known as "Raad." The lake is surrounded by Mughal-era gardens like Shalimar Bagh and Nishat Bagh, offering picturesque views.

Betaab Valley, Pahalgam

About

Betaab Valley, named after the Bollywood film "Betaab," is a picturesque location nestled between the Pir Panjal and Zanskar mountain ranges. The valley is characterized by its lush green meadows, dense vegetation, and the crystal-clear Lidder River flowing through it. It's a popular spot for trekking, camping, and photography, offering breathtaking views and a tranquil environment.

Apharwat Peak, Gulmarg

About

Apharwat Peak is a prominent mountain peak in Gulmarg, accessible via the Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world. During winter, it transforms into a skiing haven, attracting adventure enthusiasts from around the globe. The peak offers panoramic views of the surrounding snow-covered mountains and is a base for various winter sports activities.

Thajiwas Glacier, Sonmarg

About

Thajiwas Glacier is a stunning glacier located near Sonmarg, offering a serene and picturesque landscape. Visitors can embark on a short trek to reach the glacier, passing through lush meadows and streams. The glacier is a popular spot for trekking and photography, providing breathtaking views of the surrounding snow-capped mountains.

Shaliganga River, Doodhpathri

About

The Shaliganga River flows through Doodhpathri, a lesser-known alpine meadow in the Pir Panjal range. The river is known for its crystal-clear waters and serene surroundings, making it an ideal spot for picnics and nature walks. The area is also home to seasonal shepherds and offers a glimpse into the traditional pastoral lifestyle.
