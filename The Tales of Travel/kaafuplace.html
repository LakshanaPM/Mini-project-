Tourist Places in Kaafu Atoll

Things To Do

Thulusdhoo Island

About

Known as the "surf island" of the Maldives, Thulusdhoo is renowned for its world-class surf breaks like Cokes and Chickens. Beyond surfing, visitors can enjoy snorkeling, diving, and cultural experiences, including visits to local mosques and the Coca-Cola factory. The island offers a blend of adventure and authentic Maldivian life.

Meeru Island

About

Meeru Island is a private resort located on Meerufenfushi, offering a tranquil retreat with pristine beaches and clear turquoise waters. It's one of the earliest tourist resorts in the Maldives, established in 1976, and provides a range of water sports, spa services, and cultural activities.

Biyadhoo Island

About

Biyadhoo Island is a circular 10-acre resort-island in South Malé Atoll, known for its lush vegetation and clear waters. The island is a haven for diving enthusiasts, offering access to vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life.

Veligandu Island Beach

About

Veligandu Island Beach is famed for its white sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and photography. The beach offers a serene environment, ideal for unwinding and enjoying the natural beauty of the Maldives.

Banana Reef

About

Banana Reef, located in North Malé Atoll, is one of the most popular diving sites in the Maldives. The reef is known for its crescent shape, vibrant coral formations, and diverse marine life, including groupers, snappers, and moray eels.
