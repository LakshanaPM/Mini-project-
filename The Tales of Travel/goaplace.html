Tourist Places in Goa

Things To Do

The Palolem Beach

About

Palolem Beach, located in Canacona, South Goa, is renowned for its crescent-shaped shoreline, swaying palm trees, and tranquil waters. The beach offers a serene atmosphere, making it ideal for relaxation, swimming, and kayaking. Visitors can also enjoy dolphin watching, yoga sessions, and Ayurvedic massages, all set against the backdrop of stunning sunsets.

The Basilica of Bom Jesus

About

The Basilica of Bom Jesus, consecrated in 1605, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a prime example of Renaissance Baroque architecture. Located in Old Goa, it houses the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier, a co-founder of the Jesuit order. The basilica's ornate façade and interior, featuring intricate carvings and marble inlays, attract pilgrims and history enthusiasts alike.

Dudhsagar Waterfalls

About

Dudhsagar Waterfalls, situated in the Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary, is one of India's tallest waterfalls, cascading down in four tiers. The falls are surrounded by lush forests and rich biodiversity, offering a picturesque setting for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Visitors can reach the falls via a scenic trek or jeep safari, especially captivating during the monsoon season.

Chapora Fort

About

Chapora Fort, built in 1617, is perched on a hilltop overlooking the Chapora River and the Arabian Sea. The fort offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and is a popular spot for watching sunsets. Its historical significance and scenic location make it a favorite among travelers seeking a blend of history and natural beauty.

Baga Beach & Tito’s Lane

About

Baga Beach, located in North Goa, is a vibrant destination known for its lively atmosphere, water sports, and bustling nightlife. Adjacent to the beach, Tito's Lane is famous for its array of bars, clubs, and restaurants, offering a dynamic experience for those seeking entertainment and socializing.
