Tourist Places in Baa Atoll

Things To Do

Hanifaru Bay

About

A UNESCO Biosphere Reserve, Hanifaru Bay is famous for its seasonal congregation of manta rays and whale sharks. From May to November, the bay becomes a feeding hotspot as plankton-rich waters attract these majestic creatures. Snorkelers can witness mesmerizing feeding behaviors like chain and cyclone formations, with visibility often in shallow waters.

Eydhafushi Island

About

Eydhafushi is the cultural heart of Baa Atoll, offering insights into traditional Maldivian life. Visitors can explore historical landmarks, including old mosques and indigenous crafts. The island's vibrant local culture and commitment to preserving traditions make it a unique destination for cultural enthusiasts.

Dharavandhoo Island

About

Known for its strong focus on marine conservation and sustainability, Dharavandhoo Island is a popular base for diving and snorkeling enthusiasts. The local community plays a vital role in preserving the natural surroundings, particularly the coral reefs and marine life, making it an eco-friendly destination.

Four Seasons Resort at Landaa Giraavaru

About

This luxury resort offers a combination of luxury and nature, with facilities that provide a deeper connection to the ocean. Guests can engage in various activities, such as marine research and conservation programs, and enjoy wellness experiences. The resort's Marine Discovery Centre provides educational experiences about the local marine environment.

Anantara Kihavah Maldives Villas

About

Anantara Kihavah is famous for its underwater restaurant, where guests dine surrounded by marine life in clear ocean waters. The resort also has an observatory for stargazing, allowing visitors to explore the night sky through powerful telescopes. In addition to dining and stargazing, it provides wellness services such as spa treatments and yoga sessions.
