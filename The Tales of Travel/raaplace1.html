Tourist Places in Raa Atoll

Things To Do

Vaadhoo Island

About

Vaadhoo Island is famous for the bioluminescent plankton known as Redhan, which creates the mesmerizing "Sea of Stars" phenomenon. This natural light show is best observed at night, particularly between June and October. Visitors can enjoy kayaking and snorkeling during the day, and witness the glowing shoreline after sunset.

Maamigili Island

About

Maamigili is an uninhabited island in Raa Atoll, home to the Loama Resort Maldives. The resort features the Loama Museum, which houses artifacts from various historical periods, including classical, colonial, and Islamic eras. Visitors can explore ancient baths and traditional houses, gaining insight into the Maldives' rich cultural heritage.

Alifushi Island

About

Alifushi is an inhabited island known for its skilled carpenters and boat builders. The island's economy revolves around traditional craftsmanship, and visitors can observe the construction of dhonis (traditional Maldivian boats) and other wooden structures. The coral sand-covered streets add to the island's unique charm.

Sola Corner

About

Sola Corner, located southwest of Raa Atoll in the Kukulkhudhoo channel, is a popular diving site known for its cleaning station at a depth of about 15 meters. During certain seasons, manta rays gather here, offering divers the opportunity to witness these majestic creatures up close.

Reethi Thila

About

Reethi Thila is a shallow underwater pinnacle inside the lagoon, attracting a diverse array of marine life. The site features reefs, overhangs, caverns, and drop-offs, providing a habitat for various fish species, including sharks and rays. The abundance of marine life makes it a must-visit for diving enthusiasts.
